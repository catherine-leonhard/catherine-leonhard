STAMPS Wayfinding System

In a recent project, I was tasked with redesigning the wayfinding system for the Penny W. Stamps School of Art & Design at the University of Michigan. A key focus of the project was developing a cohesive solution that effectively integrated both Stamps and the Taubman College of Architecture, which share the same building.

MatchMakers

As the capstone project for a user interface development class, my group created a prototype for a roommate matching app designed to help undergraduate students find compatible roommates based on their habits, schedules, and personal preferences. During the iterative process our group utilized user-centered design strategies such as storyboarding, affinity diagrams, think aloud protocols, and thorough user testing.
