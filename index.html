About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Michigan Penny W. STAMPS School of Art & Design with a Bachelor's of Fine Arts and a minor in Computer Science. I work as a communications assistant for the Michigan Institute for Data & AI in Society where I manage and update the institute’s website, enhancing design, functionality, and user experience, guide and collaborate with staff members on best practices for website development, and ensure all web pages are consistent and align with the institution’s overall marketing goals. My professional interests include graphic design, UI/UX design, and photography. In my free time you can find me listening to music, traveling, or running at a track nearby.
